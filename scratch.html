Why I Love Mountain Biking

Mountain biking in southern Utah is unlike anything else in the world. The red rock terrain, the technical trails, and the breathtaking views make every ride an adventure. I ride a Pivot Trail 429 and primarily ride around Gooseberry Mesa and Hurricane Rim.

My Favorite Trail Systems

  1. Gooseberry Mesa
    • Technical slickrock
    • Amazing views of Zion
    • Best in spring and fall
  2. Hurricane Rim
    • Fast and flowy
    • Great for all skill levels
    • Stunning canyon views
  3. Barrel Roll
    • Technical features
    • Great progression trail
